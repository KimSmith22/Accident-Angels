  • About Michigan's Auto No-Fault Law

 

Michigan Auto No-Fault Law, MCL.500.3107, details how benefits are payable for "Allowable expenses consisting of all reasonable charges incurred for reasonable necessary products, services and accommodations for an injured person's care, recovery or rehabilitation."

     

    Some examples of products and services include:

  • Case Management Services
  • Medical Exams
  • Hospital Bills
  • X-Rays and MRI's
  • Prescription Drugs
  • Rehabilitation and other therapies
  • Wheelchairs and walkers
  • And more...

 

 

We assign you a Case Manager who will maintain constant communication with your auto insurance company to ensure you are receiving the benefits available to you under the Michigan Auto No-Fault Law. We promise to keep you informed regarding discussions with your insurance company.

 

 

Please take note: While the feedback Accident Angels provides regarding medical benefits is invaluable, it is in no way a replacement for the advice of an attorney.
