Accident Angel's vision is to make a difference in the lives of the people living in the communities in which we service. Providing assistance in local neighborhoods is an example of our commitment to enriching the lives of others. Our mission is to strenghten Metro Detroit neighborhoods through community events and fundraising initiatives, as well as sponsoring and participating in national and local events held in the Detroit Metro Area.

 

"We donate to help those in need. There are members of our team who have been in need at one time or another. It is our responsibility to lift each other up as a community. I fully believe in the idea that we are not designed to do this alone; communities need to work together in order to strengthen families."

-LaShawn Davis

CEO Accident Angels
